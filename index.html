Fun Facts

- I actually was in some productions, as Penny's Mom in Hairspray and Ensemble in Bye Bye Birdie and Cats! I've also made my own play for a playwrighting program!

- I love video games! My first game was a Naruto fighting game on the DS, and then came my Mario phase. Yikes. I tend to go for the plot-driven games with mystery/drama/a cool world-building concept, like Ace Attorney, Danganronpa, Detroit: Become Human, and more!

- I can code Python, as I have taken a class on it from Feb 2020-June 2020!

- I am learning Graphic Design right now, having some experience already, as I do it for banners/posters for an internship I'm in called Hideouts!

Favorite Food

- I have a diverse taste! I love chocolate a whole lot, but I also love savory foods! Seafood is a fav, with shrimp being the best to me! I love fettucini alfredo, and so much more! Ice cream desserts always cheer me up!

Hobbies

- I actually have been drawing ever since I was 6! I've always loved coloring (it's so calming) and now, I plan to one day get my own comic published! I already post my drawings but it's mainly for fun! I mainly do them in an anime-esque style, but do like exploring sometimes.

- I love to sing! I was in chorus ever since elementary school, and love musicals a whole lot, so singing has always been a passion. I can sing in both female and male voices (am proud of my range). I also songwrite at times!

- I definitely love making up stories and characters. I LOVE world building and coming up with concepts, and love seeing it more visually rather than in words (like in plays, or comic form).

- I watch anime! My first anime was Naruto, but from then on, I began watching more, mainstream and non-mainstream, like Bungou Stray Dogs, Durarara,Fairy Tail, and more!
